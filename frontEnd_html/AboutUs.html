About Us

Our Mission

At CarbonAware Technologies, we are committed to combating climate change by providing innovative solutions for carbon credit issuance and management. Our mission is to facilitate the transition to a sustainable future by supporting projects that reduce greenhouse gas emissions and promote environmental stewardship.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, CarbonAware Technologies emerged from a passion for environmental sustainability and a desire to make a tangible difference in the fight against climate change. Our team is dedicated to offering transparent and reliable carbon credit services to organizations and individuals worldwide.
